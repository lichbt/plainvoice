If you've ever used a "free" invoicing app, you know the script: three invoices a
month, then a paywall. A year later the price doubles. Features you relied on
move to a higher tier. One day, payment collection is switched on without asking.

We read those one-star reviews so we wouldn't repeat them. **Plainvoice is built
on a simple promise:** the core is free, forever, with no counting.

## What "free" actually means here

- **Unlimited** invoices, clients and estimates. We never count them.
- **Real PDF export** and get-paid links — not behind a wall.
- Your data lives **on your device**. One-click export, any time. No hostages.
- We never take a percentage of what your clients pay you. 0% fee.

## So how do we keep the lights on?

AI is the only thing you ever pay for, and only when you want it — describing an
invoice in a sentence, translating one into your client's language, or reading a
photo of a paper invoice. Making invoices by hand is always free.

That's the whole model. No surprises, in writing, permanently.
