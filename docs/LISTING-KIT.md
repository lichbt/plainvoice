## Descriptions (three lengths)

### One-liner
Invoicing that's actually free — unlimited invoices, no signup, no rug-pull.

### 50 words (short description / PH tagline / directory blurb)
Plainvoice is genuinely free invoicing for freelancers and small businesses.
Unlimited invoices, clients and estimates, real PDF export and get-paid links —
no account, no caps, and a written promise we'll never paywall what's free today.
Your data stays on your device. Optional AI does the typing when you want it.

### 250 words (AlternativeTo / Capterra / G2 "about" / long description)
Plainvoice is invoicing that's actually free — built for freelancers and small
businesses who are tired of apps that cap you at three invoices a month, hike the
price, or quietly turn on payment skimming.

The whole core is free, forever: unlimited invoices, clients and estimates, real
PDF export, your logo and brand colours, get-paid links (0% fee — the money goes
straight to you, we never touch it), estimates that convert to invoices, partial
payments, CSV import, and one-click data export. There's no account and no login —
your data lives on your own device (local-first), and the app installs to your
phone's home screen and works offline.

We put a Pricing Promise in writing: existing users are grandfathered on any price
change, we tell you before anything changes, and we never remove a feature you
already have.

The only thing that ever costs money is optional AI, and only when you choose to
use it: describe an invoice in a sentence and get an editable draft, snap a photo
of a paper invoice and have it rebuilt, or translate a whole invoice into your
client's language (13 supported, with localised dates and currency). Making
invoices by hand is always free.

If you've ever been pushed off a "free" invoicing tool the moment you actually
used it, Plainvoice is the honest alternative — no surprises, in writing,
permanently.
